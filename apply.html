At Aidmo, we’re transforming the way healthcare is delivered in the home. Our mission is to provide innovative, technology-driven solutions that empower patients, caregivers, and healthcare providers to improve outcomes and enhance the quality of life. As a rapidly growing home health care company, we combine cutting-edge technology with compassionate care to create an ecosystem that supports individuals at home. We’re looking for talented and driven engineers who are passionate about using technology to make a real-world impact on health and wellness.

Aidmo is seeking a Software Engineer to join our dynamic tech team and help build the next generation of home health care solutions. In this role, you’ll develop scalable, secure, and intuitive software products that improve the care experience for patients and caregivers. You’ll be part of a collaborative, remote-first environment where your contributions will have a direct impact on making healthcare more accessible and effective.

How to Apply:

If you're passionate about improving healthcare through technology and want to make a real impact, we'd love to hear from you. Please send your resume, a cover letter, and any relevant portfolio or code samples to our email.
